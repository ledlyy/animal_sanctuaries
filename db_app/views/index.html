About Us

We are a group of dedicated students who have come together for our DBMS project to create an online platform called "Animal Sanctuaries." Our mission is to promote the well-being of animals by providing a platform for animal shelters and individuals to showcase animals in need of homes, connect with potential adopters, and raise awareness about animal welfare issues.

Our Mission

At Animal Sanctuaries, our mission is to provide love, care, and protection for animals in need. We strive to connect animals with loving and responsible adopters, promote responsible pet ownership, and support animal shelters and rescue organizations in their efforts to find forever homes for abandoned and neglected animals. Through our platform, we aim to raise awareness about animal welfare issues and inspire positive change in the lives of animals.
